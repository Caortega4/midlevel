To release a new version of ppca

- update current-version.txt in this directory

- in src, rm -i *~, and other junk files

- verify no other large junk has crept in (du -sh . should be 7 MB)

- cd .. and tar cvfz ppca.tga ppca (4 megabytes)

- upload to website

Also push to github, following git-cheatsheet.txt
